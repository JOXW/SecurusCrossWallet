Welcome to Securus

This software is a platform to create and manage your own Securus-Wallet. There are many features implemented, which you can use for transactions, creating backups, sharing your public adress and getting an overview, about your last actions. You are able to choose completely free, which node you want to use to manage your transactions.

For More information about this wallet, the other software of Securus and the vision behind it, visit us on Github, or take a look on our homepage.

Thank for beeing a part of our Community!
